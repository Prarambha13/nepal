The Land of Diversity: Discover its Three Geographical Regions

Himalayan Hilly Terai

Himalayan Region

The Himalayan region lies in the northernmost part of Nepal and includes some of the highest peaks in the world. Mount Everest, the tallest mountain on Earth, is located here.This region is cold and covered with snow most of the year.It is sparsely populated, with people mainly living in small villages and practicing mountain tourism. The Himalayan region is also rich in culture, with Buddhist monasteries and Sherpa traditions.

Hilly Region

The Hilly region lies between the Himalayan region in the north and the Terai region in the south. It has a moderate climate with beautiful valleys, hills, and rivers.This region is densely populated and includes major cities like Kathmandu and Pokhara. Farming, tourism, and trade are common occupations here.The Hilly region is culturally rich, with diverse ethnic groups and traditional lifestyles.

Terai Region

The Terai region lies in the southern part of Nepal and is mostly flat and fertile.It has a hot and humid climate, ideal for agriculture.This region is known as the "grain basket" of Nepal due to its high food production.Major cities like Biratnagar, Janakpur, and Nepalgunj are located here.It is home to diverse communities and important wildlife reserves like Chitwan National Park.
